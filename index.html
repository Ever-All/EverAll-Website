About EverAll

EverAll is a powerful, platform-agnostic solution for receiving real-time trading alerts. Built by and for retail traders, we give users the ability to track top traders, receive curated trade signals, and make smarter decisions — all in one place.

Platform Highlights

Real-Time Alerts

Instant updates for trades, ideas, and market movements from vetted traders.

Personalized Subscriptions

Follow your preferred traders and get alerts tailored to their strategy.

Trader Incentives

Traders earn based on the size of their follower base, incentivizing quality alerts.

Flexible Switching

Switch between up to five traders freely to match your evolving strategy.

History

EverAll began as a Discord bot, designed to deliver real-time trade alerts inside community servers. While we’ve grown into a standalone platform with a full web app and premium service tiers, our Discord legacy remains strong. In fact, EverAll continues to support Discord integrations for alert delivery — keeping our roots connected while expanding what’s possible.
