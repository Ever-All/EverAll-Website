About EverAll

EverAll is a unique platform designed to empower traders with the ability to share real-time options alerts. Built by traders for traders, EverAll brings the latest market insights directly to your Discord server. We aim to expand into additional platforms soon, making EverAll the go-to solution for real-time trading alerts.

Core Features

Real-Time Alerts

Receive up-to-the-minute alerts for options trades from top traders across various markets.

Follow Specific Traders

Follow your favorite traders and get personalized alerts based on their strategies and trades.

Trader Payment System

Traders who apply to use EverAll can earn payment based on how many active followers they have watching their alerts.

Flexible Trader Switching

Users can switch between up to five traders at a time, with the flexibility to change traders as often as they like.
