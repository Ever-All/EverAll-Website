Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2, 2025

At EverAll, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information that you share while using our application or services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with EverAll, whether through our platform directly or via integrations such as Discord.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User-Provided Information

We may collect the following data when you sign up or interact with our services:

  • Account Details: Including email addresses, usernames, or other identifiers necessary for account creation and management.
  • Profile Information: Information you voluntarily provide such as display names, profile photos, and preferences.

1.2 Integration Data

When using EverAll through connected services (e.g., Discord), we may collect:

  • Platform Identifiers: Such as platform-specific IDs, server/guild information, roles, or interaction logs.
  • Communication Content: Limited to information required to perform the service you requested, such as command inputs or user actions.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Logs: Data on app usage, including access times, features used, and technical logs.
  • Device & Connection Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and similar metadata.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality: To provide, operate, and maintain our platform and its features.
  • Account Management: To personalize your experience and support account recovery or service preferences.
  • Diagnostics & Improvements: To monitor service performance, troubleshoot issues, and enhance functionality.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Third-Party Integrations: If you connect EverAll to other platforms (e.g., Discord), we may share necessary data for the integration to function.
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party vendors (e.g., hosting or analytics) that process data on our behalf under strict data protection agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: Data may be disclosed if required to comply with applicable laws, legal processes, or enforce our terms.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. While no system is completely secure, we strive to use industry standards to safeguard personal information.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Correction: You can request access to your data and ask us to update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You may request permanent deletion of your data. For integration-specific data, the request must come from the account holder or authorized party.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with our legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

7. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under that age. If we discover such information, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here, and your continued use of the service implies consent to the updated terms.
