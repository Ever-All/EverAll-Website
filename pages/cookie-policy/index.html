Cookie Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2025

This policy outlines EverAll's limited use of cookies, which are small data files stored on your device when you visit our website.

1. Cookie Usage Policy

EverAll operates under a strict minimal-cookie policy. We exclusively employ essential cookies required for core website functionality. We do not use any cookies for analytics, advertising, or user tracking.

2. Essential Cookies

Our essential cookies serve only the following purposes:

  • Authentication and session management
  • User preference storage (e.g., theme settings)
  • Basic security functions

3. Third-Party Services

For payment processing (e.g., PayPal), certain cookies are required to complete transactions securely. These cookies are limited to payment authentication and checkout functionality only. No third-party tracking or advertising cookies are allowed.

3.1 PayPal Integration

When using PayPal for payments, their cookies are necessary to:

  • Process secure transactions
  • Prevent fraudulent activities
  • Complete checkout procedures

Please note that PayPal's own cookie policy applies to their services. We recommend reviewing their privacy and cookie policies for more details.

4. Cookie Management

Users maintain full control over cookies through their browser settings:

  • View and manage stored cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Configure cookie permissions

Note: Blocking essential cookies may impair website functionality.

5. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect operational or legal requirements, always maintaining our commitment to minimal cookie usage.
